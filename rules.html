Scoring rules

Points escalate round by round — the same rules the brackets were filled out under on defirate.com. Maximum possible: 360 points.

  • Group stage: 2 points for each team you correctly placed in the top two of its group (your 1st/2nd order doesn't matter).
  • Best thirds: 3 points for each picked third-place team that really finishes 3rd in its group and advances among the 8 best thirds.
  • Knockouts: you score a match when the team you picked wins that exact match — if your team isn't even in it, the pick is simply wrong.
  • Possible (on the leaderboard) shows each player's ceiling if everything else breaks their way from here.

How this site works

  • All 7 brackets were locked on defirate.com at noon on June 11, 2026 — what you see here is everyone's final picks.
  • Results sync automatically from the tournament feed about every 30 minutes; group tables, the best-thirds ranking and the knockout bracket all fill in by themselves.
  • Group tiebreakers: points, then goal difference, then goals scored, then head-to-head among the tied teams.
