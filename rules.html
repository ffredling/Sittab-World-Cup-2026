Rules

How points are scored — 360 on the line.

We score every bracket the same way: predict each stage of the tournament and earn points as the real results come in. The deeper your call holds up, the more it is worth.

  • Results update automatically as matches finish — a new score usually shows within a few hours.
  • Group standings and the best third-placed teams settle once every group has played.
  • Group stage: 2 points for each team you correctly placed in the top two of its group — your 1st/2nd order doesn't matter.
  • Best thirds: 3 points for each picked third-place team that really finishes 3rd in its group and advances among the 8 best.
  • Knockouts: you score a match when the team you picked wins that exact match — Round of 32 is 4 points, then 8, 16, 24 per round, 16 for the bronze final and 32 for the final.
  • Players tied on total points share the same rank.
